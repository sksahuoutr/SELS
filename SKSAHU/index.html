Professional Summary

Prof. Sahu is dedicated to electronics and communication, with a strong focus on teaching, research, and academic mentorship. He follows a holistic approach that combines theoretical learning with practical engineering applications to build well-rounded professionals.

At OUTR, he works closely with students and faculty to foster innovation and research culture. His primary interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Biomedical Signal Analysis for solving real-world problems.
